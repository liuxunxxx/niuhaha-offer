Free Checklist: 5 places training schools lose leads

Use this before buying tools or asking staff to send more messages. It focuses on public-safe workflow gaps that can be fixed without scraping private data or making enrollment promises.

  1. New inquiries do not have a visible owner.
  2. Trial-class interest is mixed with general FAQ messages.
  3. Price and package questions are answered differently by each staff member.
  4. Follow-up timing depends on memory instead of reminders.
  5. Weekly review looks at total leads but not stage movement.

If the checklist matches your situation, submit diagnostic, sprint, or maintenance interest through the public-safe intake form.
