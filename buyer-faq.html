Buyer FAQ

Answers for training businesses that need a public-safe lead follow-up workflow before sharing private data, signing a contract, or approving payment.

  • What data is required?
    Start with public information or buyer-approved sample data. Do not post private student data, passwords, payment details, or non-public customer records in public intake.
  • What outcomes are promised?
    No enrollment, conversion, or revenue outcomes are promised. The deliverable is workflow setup, documentation, reply drafts, reminders, and review support.
  • When can payment be requested?
    Seller, invoice, and payment setup must be verified before payment requests. Diagnostic, sprint, or maintenance interest starts with manual review.
  • How does a request start?
    Submit order interest through the public-safe intake form, then keep sensitive billing, contract, and internal data out of public comments.
