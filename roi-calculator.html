ROI and payback worksheet

Use public-safe business assumptions to decide whether a paid diagnostic or sprint setup is worth discussing. This worksheet is not a forecast, not buyer-specific advice, and not income evidence.

  • Missed follow-up value
    Estimate monthly inquiries, the share that currently go stale, and the average gross value of one won customer from your own records.
  • Simple payback estimate
    Compare one saved customer or one reduced admin bottleneck with the RMB 1,999 diagnostic entry point and the fixed-scope sprint discussion.
  • Decision rule
    If one plausible retained customer or reduced staff bottleneck could exceed the diagnostic fee, submit public-safe order interest for manual review.
  • Evidence boundary
    No enrollment, conversion, or revenue outcomes are promised. Use this only to frame a buying conversation before any contract or payment step.
