# Static Site Publish Checklist

The static site package is not publication evidence and is not income.

Before publishing:

1. Confirm the publishing account or domain is authorized for this business.
2. Replace `ORDER_INTAKE_ENDPOINT_REQUIRED` with an authorized form endpoint.
3. Confirm the endpoint exports rows compatible with `operations/inbound-submissions.initial.csv`.
4. Confirm privacy notice, retention, deletion path, and access controls.
5. Store publication URL, timestamp, reviewer, and platform as evidence before updating the publish queue.

Revenue boundary: `actual_net_rmb remains 0` until payment evidence and direct delivery cost evidence exist.
