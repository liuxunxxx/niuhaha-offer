7-Day Sprint Workspace Demo

Preview the fixed-scope workspace a training business receives after manual contract review. This public demo uses placeholder rows only. No private student data, passwords, payment data, or internal customer records are required.

  • Lead intake schema
    Fields for source, channel, course interest, stage, owner, last touchpoint, next action date, and authorization note.
  • Sales stage board
    Stages from new inquiry to qualified, trial booked, post-trial follow-up, proposal sent, won, and lost.
  • FAQ library
    Approved-answer rows for price, trial booking, teacher background, and refund questions before staff reply.
  • Follow-up reminders
    Same-day inquiry response, next-business-day qualified follow-up, pre-trial reminder, and proposal follow-up rules.
  • Weekly review
    Review channel quality, stage drop-off, repeated questions, missed reminders, and policy approvals.
  • Staff handoff
    Daily operating steps, escalation boundaries, and compliance guardrails for the workflow owner.

No revenue or conversion guarantees. The demo shows workflow assets, not buyer-specific diagnosis, enrollment promises, or payment evidence.
